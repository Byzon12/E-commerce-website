A payment reference, also known as a transaction reference or payment ID, is a unique identifier assigned to a financial transaction. It helps both the sender and the receiver identify and track the payment. This reference typically accompanies the transfer of funds and is often provided by the payer to ensure that their payment is properly credited to the intended recipient.The format and usage of payment references can vary depending on the payment method and the financial institution involved. For example, when making online payments or bank transfers, you may be asked to provide a payment reference to link the payment to a specific invoice, order, or account.
